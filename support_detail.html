Simple Event Creation

Creating an event on Axesum is a streamlined process designed to help you get your event up and running with minimal hassle. Follow this detailed guide to create a successful event that attracts and engages attendees.

In this article:
1. Go to Manage my events. 2. Select 'Create Event'. 3. Enter event details. 4. Choose event type and category. 5. Set up tickets. 6. Customize your event page. 7. Publish your event. 8. Promote your event.
Step-by-Step Guide:
Go to Manage My Events

Navigating to the "Manage My Events" section is your first step toward organizing a successful event. This centralized hub provides all the tools you need to oversee your events, from creation to promotion. It's your command center, where you can track progress, make adjustments, and ensure everything runs smoothly.

Select Create Event

Once in the "Manage My Events" section, select the "Create Event" option to begin building your event from the ground up. This user-friendly feature allows you to start crafting your event with just a click, guiding you through the process with ease, ensuring nothing is overlooked.

Enter Event Details

In the event creation process, entering the event details is crucial. Here, you’ll provide all the essential information such as the event name, date, location, and a brief description. These details help attendees understand what to expect, setting the tone and giving your event its unique identity.

Choose Event Type and Category

Next, categorize your event by selecting the appropriate type and category. Whether it's a conference, workshop, or social gathering, accurately classifying your event helps potential attendees find it more easily and allows for better-targeted promotion.

Set Up Tickets

Setting up tickets is where you define how attendees will participate. Whether you’re offering free entry, paid tickets, or early bird specials, this step allows you to configure pricing, availability, and ticket types. It ensures a smooth registration process, tailored to your audience’s needs.

Customize Your Event Page

Your event page is the digital face of your event. Here, you can customize it to reflect your branding, add images, videos, and key information that will engage and attract attendees. A well-designed event page makes a strong first impression and can significantly influence the success of your event.

Publish Your Event

After finalizing all details, it’s time to publish your event. This step makes your event live and visible to the public. Your event is now officially open for registration, and attendees can start signing up, marking the beginning of your journey to event success.

Promote Your Event

With your event live, promoting it is crucial to maximize attendance. Utilize social media, email campaigns, and other marketing tools to spread the word. Effective promotion drives interest and engagement, ensuring your event reaches the right audience and achieves its goals..

Tips for Successful Events:

  • Engage Early: Start promoting your event as soon as it’s live to build momentum and secure early registrations.
  • Communicate Regularly: Keep your attendees informed with updates and reminders leading up to the event.
  • Use Analytics: Utilize Axesum’s analytics tools to track registrations and engagement. Adjust your promotional strategies based on these insights.
