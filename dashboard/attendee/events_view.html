About this event
Welcome to Ayurveda for Self Healing -Level 1 Training, where you will unlock the secrets of Ayurveda to transform your life!

Our Ayurveda Self-Healing Level 1 Training is designed to introduce you to the fundamental principles of Ayurveda. This training will empower you to identify your dosha (body constitution), understand your gunas (qualities), and make informed lifestyle choices. You will learn practical tips and guidelines for a balanced life, tailored to your individual needs based on your body type, location, age, gender, occupation, and more.

In this training, you will learn the basics of Ayurveda, including doshas, daily routines, and self-care practices. You'll also get practical tips on how to incorporate Ayurvedic principles into your daily life for improved health and well-being.

Whether you're new to Ayurveda or looking to deepen your understanding, this training is perfect for anyone interested in natural healing and self-care. Get ready to embark on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment with Ayurveda!

Curriculum:

1. Introduction to Ayurveda: Philosophy, Core Concepts and Principles

2. Understanding Doshas: Vata, Pitta, Kapha (Characteristics and Influences); How to Identify Your Dosha

3. Personal Qualities and Influences: Guna (Sattva, Rajas, Tamas)

4. Self-Diagnosis Techniques: Physical, Mental, and Emotional Indicators

5. Lifestyle Modifications

- Dos and Don'ts for Each Dosha

- Simple Changes for Immediate Benefits

- Desha: Geographic Influence on Health

Highlights:

- Interactive and engaging sessions

- Personalized guidance from expert practitioners
