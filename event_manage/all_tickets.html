Please provide a PNG or JPEG image of the site map. Preferably with the following characteristics:

  • Minimum resolution: 1024x1024 pixels.
  • Max size: 100 MB
  • Clear and legible zone/section names.
  • Division into different sections (Center, Left, etc.).
  • Please do not include ticket prices or event-specific details so that the venue template can be reused for other events.
  • The image you upload must be legible and well resented.
  • You can use colors for each zone/section.
  • Axesum may reject maps that do not meet the necessary quality standards.
